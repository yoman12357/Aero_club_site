About Us

Founded by a group of aviation enthusiasts at NITK Surathkal, Aero NITK is the official aerospace and aeromodelling club of the institute. We provide a platform for students to explore the world of flight â€” from fundamental principles of aerodynamics to cutting-edge UAV and drone technologies.

Our club functions as a blend of engineering creativity and teamwork. We engage in designing, fabricating, and flying various aerospace systems, ranging from gliders and quadcopters to autonomous drones. Students gain hands-on experience in 3D designing, CAD modeling, circuit integration, and control systems.

Aero NITK regularly represents the institute at prestigious national-level competitions such as SAE Aero Design, Techkriti, and Boeing Aeromodelling. We also conduct workshops, seminars, and flying events for the student community to inspire interest in aerospace engineering.
